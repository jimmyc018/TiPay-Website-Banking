Report a card lost, Stolen or Damaged

How to report your card lost, stolen or damaged.

Misplaced:

Add a temporary lock on your card while you look for it

  1. Log on to TiPay
  2. Go to settings
  3. Under card security select Lost, Stolen and Damaged Card
  4. Select Misplace Card then click Confirm

Lost or Stolen:

Cancel your card and order a replacement

  1. Log on to TiPay
  2. Go to settings
  3. Under card security select Lost, Stolen and Damaged Card
  4. Select Lost or Stolen then click Confirm

Damaged:

Order a replacement card. The card number will be different

  1. Log on to TiPay
  2. Go to settings
  3. Under card security select Lost, Stolen and Damaged Card
  4. Select Damaged Card then click Confirm
